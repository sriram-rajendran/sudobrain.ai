# Code Of Conduct

SudoBrain is built for private work knowledge, so community participation should
be careful, respectful, and security-minded.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially when discussing privacy or safety tradeoffs.
- Keep examples synthetic unless a maintainer explicitly asks for real data.
- Report security concerns privately through the process in `SECURITY.md`.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private user, customer, employee, meeting, email, token, or source data.
- Pressuring contributors to share credentials, logs, transcripts, or recordings.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers redirect them.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block accounts; or
take other reasonable action to keep the project safe and useful.
