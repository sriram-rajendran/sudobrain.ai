Seven Senses

One brain. Seven senses.

SudoBrain extends your cognitive capabilities across seven intelligent pillars, working together as one unified system.

Ears — Capture

Records meetings, voice notes, and system audio. Transcribes Tamil, English, and code-mixed speech with Sarvam AI. Your words, perfectly preserved.

Eyes — Context

Sees your active app, calendar, focus mode, and time of day. Knows when to help and when to stay silent. Context-aware, not context-blind.

Brain — Understanding

Extracts decisions, action items, people, and promises from everything you capture. Connects dots you didn't know existed.

Memory — Knowledge

Temporal knowledge graph with semantic search. Three-tier hot/warm/cold architecture. Your personal Wikipedia that grows smarter daily.

Mouth — Communication

Morning briefings, meeting prep, proactive alerts. Speaks up when something important needs attention. Silent when it doesn't.

Hands — Actions

Drafts follow-ups, prepares calendar notes, and keeps reminders local. Turns thoughts into completed actions.

Mirror — Insights

Time allocation, relationship health, productivity patterns, work-life balance. See yourself clearly through your own data.

How It Works

Three steps. Zero friction.

SudoBrain runs quietly in your menu bar. Always ready, never intrusive.

1

Capture

Click the menu bar icon or press a hotkey. SudoBrain records audio, captures clipboard and screen context. Sarvam AI transcribes Tamil, English, and mixed speech in real-time.

2

Understand

Your local AI engine extracts decisions, action items, people, and promises. Builds your knowledge graph. Detects contradictions. Scores meeting effectiveness. All on your machine.

3

Act

Get morning briefings. Search your memory. Let SudoBrain create events, draft emails, send follow-ups, and surface warnings — all with tiered permissions you control.

Built Different

Your data. Your machine. Your rules.

Local-First

All data stays on your Mac. SQLite database, local audio files, on-device AI. Your thoughts never leave your machine.

Zero API Cost

AI runs through your configured local model provider. Keep data local, avoid usage meters, and review extracted knowledge before acting on it.

Self-Improving

Every correction becomes a learned rule. After 6 months, 500+ personal preferences absorbed. Gets smarter through use, not updates.

The Sudo Principle

4-tier permission model. Reads are free. Writes need soft approval. External actions need hard approval. Deletions are forbidden.
