Core Pillars

Six senses. One brain.

SudoBrain extends your cognitive capabilities across six intelligent pillars, working together as one unified system.

👂

Ears — Capture

Records meetings, voice notes, and system audio. Transcribes Tamil, English, and code-mixed speech with Sarvam AI.

👁

Eyes — Context

Sees your active app, calendar, focus mode, and time of day. Knows when to help and when to stay silent.

🧠

Brain — Understanding

Extracts decisions, action items, people, and promises from everything you capture. Connects the dots automatically.

💾

Memory — Knowledge

Temporal knowledge graph with semantic search. Your personal Wikipedia that grows smarter every day.

💬

Mouth — Communication

Morning briefings, meeting prep, proactive alerts. Speaks up when something important needs your attention.

✍️

Hands — Actions

Drafts follow-ups, prepares calendar notes, and keeps reminders local. Turns thoughts into actions.

How It Works

Three steps. Zero friction.

SudoBrain runs quietly in your menu bar, always ready but never intrusive.

1

Capture Everything

Click the menu bar icon to start recording. SudoBrain captures audio, clipboard, and screen context in the background.

2

AI Processes It

Your local AI engine transcribes, extracts insights, and builds your knowledge graph — all on your machine.

3

Act on Insights

Get briefings, search your memory, and let SudoBrain handle follow-ups, reminders, and messages automatically.

Built Different

🤖

Proactive Intelligence
